About Our School

Founded in 1995, Rthilaa High School has been providing quality education to students in our community for over 25 years.

Our Mission

To empower students to achieve academic excellence, develop strong character, and become responsible global citizens.

Our Achievements

98% graduation rate with 85% of graduates attending college. Ranked in the top 10% of high schools statewide.

Our Community

A diverse student body of 1,200 students supported by 85 dedicated faculty members and staff.

Our Campus

Our 25 acre campus features state of the art facilities, including modern classrooms, science labs, a performing arts center, and athletic fields.

Meet Our Faculty

Meet the talented faculty members who inspire and educate our students.

Dr. Sarah Johnson – Biology

Dr. Johnson brings over 15 years of research and teaching experience. She inspires students to explore the wonders of life sciences with curiosity and depth.

Mr. James Wilson – Mathematics

Mr. Wilson believes that math is a language of logic and beauty. His clear explanations and friendly approach make complex topics feel simple.

Mr. Ahmed Rahimi – Computer Science

Tech-savvy and innovative, Mr. Rahimi introduces students to coding, algorithms, and the future of technology in a fun and engaging way.

Ms. Emily Rodriguez – English Literature

A passionate reader and writer, Ms. Rodriguez encourages students to connect emotionally with literature and express themselves creatively.

Dr. Mark Thompson – Chemistry

Dr. Thompson's love for chemistry is contagious. He motivates students through hands-on labs and real-life examples of chemical reactions.

Mrs. Olivia Bennett – History

Mrs. Bennett brings the past to life with engaging stories and critical thinking exercises that help students understand the present.

Ms. Amina El-Mansouri – French Language

Originally from Morocco, Ms. El-Mansouri brings cultural richness and passion to her French classes, helping students speak with confidence.

Mr. David Chen – Physics

With a calm and focused style, Mr. Chen helps students see the real-world applications of physics through exciting experiments.
